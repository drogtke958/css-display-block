My Favorite Project

My favorite project in this class was probably the Cupcake Boss project because it was fun to go try a bunch of cupcakes and build our own webpage about it. The cupcakes were all really good and every group was very creative with their themes. For the project part, we got to judge the cupcakes based on certain categories, like taste, creativity, and the group's flyers. After, we got put in groups and created a webpage about the event. We also got to know who won each category after the whole event was done.
